The example in this floder support ArduinoUNO and Mega2560 ,or any other Arduino Boards whit the same pin-out.

Step1:you should install the <UTFT> libiray in floder "Install libraries".
Step2:directly insert your LCD into the ArduinoBoard as the Pin-map bellow:

|-------------------------|
|LCD        |	ArduinoUNO|
|-----------|-------------|
|VCC:	    |	+5V       |
|GND:	    |	GND       |
|GND:	    |	GND       |
|NC:	    |	VIN       |
|NC:	    |	NC        |
|NC:	    |	A0        |
|CLK/SCK:   |	A1    	  |
|SDA/MOSI:  |	A2        |
|RS/DC:	    |	A3        |
|RST:	    |	A4        |
|CS:	    |	A5        |

Step3:Dowload the examples to your Arduino board.
Enjoy The colorful world with your new LCD!
